# Product Launch
> **Teams:** Intelligence, Strategy, Content, Marketing | **Estimated steps:** 11

## What You'll Get
- Market research brief
- Competitive landscape summary
- Positioning and messaging document
- Launch content calendar
- 3 blog posts (announcement, deep-dive, use case)
- 30 social media posts across platforms
- 5-email launch sequence
- Google Search + Meta ad copy (10 ads)
- Landing page copy
- Press release (AP style)
- Day-by-day launch calendar

## Prerequisites
- Active client profile in vault (run `onboard` first)

## Steps

### Step 1: Market Research
**Read:** `agents/intelligence/market-researcher.md`
**Do:** Research the target market — size, trends, customer pain points, buying behavior. Use web search for real, current data.
**Save:** `vault/03-Research/[client]/market-research-[date].md`

### Step 2: Competitor Analysis
**Read:** `agents/intelligence/competitor-monitor.md`
**Do:** Analyze top 5 competitors — positioning, pricing, messaging, strengths, weaknesses. Pull from `vault/01-Clients/[client]/competitors.md` for the competitor list.
**Save:** `vault/04-Intelligence/[client]/competitor-analysis-[date].md`

### Step 3: Positioning Strategy
**Read:** `agents/strategy/growth-strategist.md`
**Do:** Define unique value proposition, positioning statement, key messages, and differentiators. Use market research and competitor analysis as inputs.
**Save:** `vault/07-Strategy/[client]/positioning-[date].md`

### Step 4: Content Plan
**Read:** `agents/content/content-calendar-planner.md`
**Do:** Plan all content needed for launch with timeline and assignments. Map each deliverable to a launch phase (pre-launch, launch day, post-launch).
**Save:** `vault/05-Content/[client]/launch-content-plan-[date].md`

### Step 5: Blog Posts (3)
**Read:** `agents/content/blog-writer.md`
**Do:** Write 3 blog posts — announcement post, problem/solution deep-dive, and customer use case story. Each 1,200-1,800 words with SEO optimization.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/product-launch/blogs/`

### Step 6: Social Media Posts (30)
**Read:** `agents/marketing/social-media/content-scheduler.md`
**Do:** Create 30 social posts across LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, and Facebook. Mix of teaser, announcement, feature highlight, and social proof formats.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/product-launch/social-posts.md`

### Step 7: Email Sequence (5)
**Read:** `agents/marketing/email/campaign-builder.md`
**Do:** Write 5-email launch sequence — teaser, announcement, features deep-dive, social proof/testimonials, last chance/urgency. Each with subject line, preview text, body, and CTA.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/product-launch/email-sequence.md`

### Step 8: Ad Copy (Google + Meta)
**Read:** `agents/marketing/ads/google-copywriter.md`
**Do:** Write 5 Google Search ads and 5 Meta ads. Each with headline variants, body copy, and CTA variations. Include targeting recommendations.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/product-launch/ad-copy.md`

### Step 9: Landing Page
**Read:** `agents/marketing/landing-pages/page-copywriter.md`
**Do:** Write full landing page copy — hero headline, subhead, benefits section, features with descriptions, testimonial placeholders, FAQ, and CTA sections.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/product-launch/landing-page.md`

### Step 10: Press Release
**Read:** `agents/content/copywriter.md`
**Do:** Write press release following AP style using `templates/press-release.md`. Include quotes, company boilerplate, and media contact section.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/product-launch/press-release.md`

### Step 11: Launch Calendar
**Read:** `agents/content/content-calendar-planner.md`
**Do:** Create day-by-day launch calendar mapping all deliverables from Steps 5-10 to specific dates, channels, and responsible teams. Cover pre-launch (2 weeks), launch day, and post-launch (2 weeks).
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/product-launch/launch-calendar.md`

### Final Delivery
1. Run quality gate (`quality/qa-checklist.md`) on every deliverable
2. Save all deliverables to `output/[client]/[date]/product-launch/`
3. Update `vault/00-Dashboard/Agency Dashboard.md` with campaign summary
4. Update `vault/00-Dashboard/ROI Tracker.md` — log 3 blogs, 30 social posts, 5 emails, 10 ads, landing page, press release, calendar
