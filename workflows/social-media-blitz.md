# Social Media Blitz
> **Teams:** Marketing (Social Media, Community), Content, Intelligence, Strategy | **Estimated steps:** 7

## What You'll Get
- Platform-specific strategy for each active channel
- Social audience insights and behavior research
- 4-5 content pillars with posting ratios
- 30 ready-to-post social media posts across platforms
- Hashtag playbook with branded, industry, and trending tags
- Daily engagement plan with templates
- Community management guidelines with escalation matrix

## Prerequisites
- Active client profile in vault (run `onboard` first)

## Steps

### Step 1: Platform Strategy
**Read:** `agents/marketing/social-media/content-scheduler.md`
**Do:** Define platform-specific strategy for each active channel (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok). For each: audience demographics, top-performing content types, posting frequency, best times, tone adjustments, and growth tactics.
**Save:** `vault/02-Campaigns/[client]/social-strategy-[date].md`

### Step 2: Audience Research
**Read:** `agents/intelligence/social-listener.md`
**Do:** Research target audience on social — what they discuss, which posts get engagement, common questions, hashtags they use, influencers they follow, and communities they belong to. Search Reddit, Twitter, and LinkedIn.
**Save:** `vault/03-Research/[client]/social-audience-[date].md`

### Step 3: Content Pillars
**Read:** `agents/strategy/growth-strategist.md`
**Do:** Define 4-5 content pillars — recurring themes aligned with brand and audience interests. For each: description, example topics, content formats, and posting ratio (e.g., 30% educational, 25% behind-scenes, 20% promotional, 15% engagement, 10% curated).
**Save:** `vault/05-Content/[client]/content-pillars-[date].md`

### Step 4: Create 30 Posts
**Read:** `agents/content/social-content-writer.md`
**Do:** Write 30 social media posts distributed across pillars and platforms: 10 LinkedIn (text, carousel outlines, polls), 10 Twitter/X (threads, tweets, quote-tweet ideas), 10 Instagram (feed captions, stories, reels ideas). Each post includes platform, content, hashtags, media suggestion, and best posting time. Use `templates/social-media-batch.md`.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/social-media-blitz/social-posts.md`

### Step 5: Hashtag Strategy
**Read:** `agents/marketing/social-media/content-scheduler.md`
**Do:** Build hashtag playbook per platform: 10 branded hashtags, 20 industry hashtags ranked by reach, 10 community hashtags, 5 trending hashtags to watch. Include platform-specific counts (LinkedIn: 3-5, Instagram: 15-25, Twitter: 2-3).
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/social-media-blitz/hashtag-strategy.md`

### Step 6: Engagement Plan
**Read:** `agents/marketing/community/manager.md`
**Do:** Create daily engagement playbook: comment strategy (accounts to engage with, conversation starters), DM outreach templates for warm leads, community participation plan, UGC encouragement tactics, and response templates for common questions.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/social-media-blitz/engagement-plan.md`

### Step 7: Community Guidelines
**Read:** `agents/marketing/community/manager.md`
**Do:** Create community management guidelines: response time targets per platform, tone guide for different situations (praise, complaints, questions, trolls), escalation matrix, crisis response templates, and banned topics/sensitivities.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/social-media-blitz/community-guidelines.md`

### Final Delivery
1. Run quality gate (`quality/qa-checklist.md`) on all deliverables
2. Save all deliverables to `output/[client]/[date]/social-media-blitz/`
3. Update `vault/00-Dashboard/Agency Dashboard.md` with social campaign summary
4. Update `vault/00-Dashboard/ROI Tracker.md` — log 30 social posts, strategy docs, hashtag playbook, engagement plan
