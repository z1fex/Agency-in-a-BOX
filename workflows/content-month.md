# Content Month
> **Teams:** Intelligence, Content, Marketing | **Estimated steps:** 7

## What You'll Get
- Trend report with 10 topic opportunities
- 30-day content calendar
- 4 long-form blog posts (1,200-1,800 words each)
- 30 social media posts across platforms
- 4 weekly newsletters
- 2 video scripts (3-5 minutes each)
- Content repurposing plan with transformation instructions

## Prerequisites
- Active client profile in vault (run `onboard` first)

## Steps

### Step 1: Trend Research
**Read:** `agents/intelligence/trend-detector.md`
**Do:** Research current trends in the client's industry using web search, Reddit, industry publications, and competitor content. Identify 10 trending topics ranked by relevance and timeliness.
**Save:** `vault/03-Research/[client]/trends-[date].md`

### Step 2: Content Calendar
**Read:** `agents/content/content-calendar-planner.md`
**Do:** Plan the full month — assign topics to formats, set publishing dates, map content to buyer journey stages, identify repurposing opportunities. Use `templates/content-calendar.md`.
**Save:** `vault/05-Content/[client]/calendar-[month]-[year].md`

### Step 3: Blog Posts (4)
**Read:** `agents/content/blog-writer.md`
**Do:** Write 4 long-form blog posts (1,200-1,800 words each). Each includes: title, meta description, H2/H3 headers, body, internal link suggestions, and CTA. Integrate SEO keywords naturally.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/content-month/blogs/`

### Step 4: Social Media Posts (30)
**Read:** `agents/content/social-content-writer.md`
**Do:** Create 30 social posts — mix of educational, promotional, engagement, and repurposed blog content. Tailored per platform (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram). Use `templates/social-media-batch.md`.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/content-month/social-posts.md`

### Step 5: Newsletters (4)
**Read:** `agents/content/newsletter-writer.md`
**Do:** Write 4 weekly newsletters with curated content, original insights, blog highlights, and CTA. Include subject lines and preview text for each.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/content-month/newsletters/`

### Step 6: Video Scripts (2)
**Read:** `agents/content/video-scriptwriter.md`
**Do:** Write 2 video scripts (3-5 minutes each) based on the top-performing blog topics. Include: hook, intro, body sections, CTA, B-roll suggestions, and on-screen text notes.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/content-month/video-scripts/`

### Step 7: Repurposing Plan
**Read:** `agents/content/content-repurposer.md`
**Do:** Map how each piece of content can be repurposed. Blog to carousel, newsletter to thread, video to short clips, blog stats to infographic. Create a repurposing matrix with specific instructions.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/content-month/repurposing-plan.md`

### Final Delivery
1. Run quality gate (`quality/qa-checklist.md`) on every deliverable
2. Save all deliverables to `output/[client]/[date]/content-month/`
3. Update `vault/00-Dashboard/Agency Dashboard.md` with content summary
4. Update `vault/00-Dashboard/ROI Tracker.md` — log 4 blogs, 30 social posts, 4 newsletters, 2 video scripts, 1 repurposing plan
