# Full Strategy
> **Teams:** Direction, Strategy, Intelligence, Marketing, Sales, Content | **Estimated steps:** 10

## What You'll Get
- OKR framework with 3-5 objectives and measurable key results
- Comprehensive SWOT analysis with strategic implications
- Market research report with TAM/SAM/SOM
- Competitive landscape analysis (top 5 competitors)
- Growth strategy with acquisition channels and retention plan
- Marketing strategy with channel and budget recommendations
- Sales strategy with pipeline design and outreach playbook
- Content strategy with pillars, cadence, and distribution plan
- 90-day execution plan broken into 3 monthly sprints
- KPI dashboard framework with baselines and targets

## Prerequisites
- Active client profile in vault (run `onboard` first)

## Steps

### Step 1: Define OKRs
**Read:** `agents/direction/goal-setter.md`
**Do:** Set 3-5 Objectives with 3-4 Key Results each. Ensure objectives are ambitious but achievable, key results are measurable with specific numeric targets. Align with business stage and available resources.
**Save:** `vault/07-Strategy/[client]/okrs-[date].md`

### Step 2: SWOT Analysis
**Read:** `agents/strategy/swot-analyst.md`
**Do:** Comprehensive SWOT — internal strengths/weaknesses (team, product, resources, brand) and external opportunities/threats (market, competitors, regulation, technology). Cross-reference for SO/WO/ST/WT strategies. Use `templates/swot-analysis.md`.
**Save:** `vault/07-Strategy/[client]/swot-[date].md`

### Step 3: Market Research
**Read:** `agents/intelligence/market-researcher.md`
**Do:** Deep market research — TAM/SAM/SOM, growth rate, market trends, customer segments, buying behavior, regulatory environment, and technology shifts. Use web search for current data.
**Save:** `vault/03-Research/[client]/market-research-[date].md`

### Step 4: Competitive Analysis
**Read:** `agents/intelligence/competitor-monitor.md`
**Do:** Analyze top 5 competitors — positioning, market share estimates, product offerings, pricing, go-to-market strategy, content strategy, and recent moves (funding, hires, launches).
**Save:** `vault/04-Intelligence/[client]/competitive-analysis-[date].md`

### Step 5: Growth Strategy
**Read:** `agents/strategy/growth-strategist.md`
**Do:** Define growth strategy — growth model (product-led, sales-led, community-led), growth levers, acquisition channels ranked by potential, retention strategy, expansion strategy, and key experiments to run.
**Save:** `vault/07-Strategy/[client]/growth-strategy-[date].md`

### Step 6: Marketing Strategy
**Read:** `agents/marketing/_lead.md`
**Do:** Define marketing strategy — positioning, messaging framework, channel strategy (organic + paid), content strategy overview, brand strategy, budget allocation, and martech stack recommendations. Use `templates/marketing-strategy.md`.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/full-strategy/marketing-strategy.md`

### Step 7: Sales Strategy
**Read:** `agents/sales/_lead.md`
**Do:** Define sales strategy — sales model, target deal size, sales cycle, pipeline stages, lead qualification criteria (BANT/MEDDIC), outreach playbook, proposal strategy, partnership channels, and revenue targets by quarter.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/full-strategy/sales-strategy.md`

### Step 8: Content Strategy
**Read:** `agents/content/_lead.md`
**Do:** Define content strategy — content mission statement, audience personas mapped to content, content pillars, channel-content matrix, formats, publishing cadence, distribution plan, repurposing framework, and measurement plan.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/full-strategy/content-strategy.md`

### Step 9: 90-Day Execution Plan
**Read:** `agents/direction/priority-manager.md`
**Do:** Create 90-day plan in 3 monthly sprints: Month 1 (Foundation) — setup, quick wins, baselines. Month 2 (Build) — campaign launches, content production, pipeline building. Month 3 (Scale) — optimization, scaling winners, cutting losers. Each month: tasks, owners, deadlines, dependencies, success criteria.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/full-strategy/90-day-plan.md`

### Step 10: KPI Dashboard
**Read:** `agents/marketing/analytics/report-generator.md`
**Do:** Design KPI dashboard — metrics for each strategy area, baselines and targets, tracking cadence (daily/weekly/monthly), reporting format, and alert thresholds. Use `templates/weekly-report.md` and `templates/monthly-report.md`.
**Save:** `output/[client]/[date]/full-strategy/kpi-dashboard.md`

### Final Delivery
1. Run quality gate (`quality/qa-checklist.md`) on all deliverables
2. Save all deliverables to `output/[client]/[date]/full-strategy/`
3. Update `vault/00-Dashboard/Agency Dashboard.md` with strategy package summary
4. Update `vault/00-Dashboard/ROI Tracker.md` — log full strategy package (OKRs, SWOT, market research, competitive analysis, 4 strategies, 90-day plan, KPI dashboard)
